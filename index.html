DWARVES       


 Stout, honor-driven, decisive, and gifted craftsmen. (p19)
DWARF TRAITS (p21)
All Dwarves have improved vision in low light/darkness, skill in a trade, training with a dwarven weapon, and are less vunerable to poisoning and resistant against poison damage.
Hill Dwarf (p21)
Friendlier and more integrated into mixed society. Really like gold.
+2 CON  +1 WIS  +1 CHA  +1 HP per level
Mountain Dwarf (p21)
Taller. More warlike. Suspicious of Elves, hate Orcs, and bonkers for gold.
+2 CON  +2 STR
· light + medium armor training

 ELVES       


Agile, enthralling, keen-eyed, and sharp-minded. Frequently act with aloofness or indiference towards other races, especially shorter-lived ones like Humans and Dragonborn. Not uncommon for them to get along well with Halflings and Gnomes, or to have a respectfully adversarial relationship with Dwarves. (p22)
ELF TRAITS (p24)
All Elves have improved vision in low light/darkness, heightened senses of awareness, reduced vulnerability to mind altering magic effects, and require only 4 hours of meditation per day instead of sleep.
High Elf (p24)
Secluded, and sometimes completely isolationist. Typically cordial and gracious, but almost universally arrogant.
+2 DEX  +1 INT
· 1 wizard spell (cantrip) that uses INT
· long/shortsword, long/shortbow training
Wood Elf (p25)
Secluded, reclusive, and highly suspicious of all non-Elves. Shown respect, they tend on the whole to be extraordinarily decent and willing to cooperate for the greater good.
+2 DEX  +1 WIS
· faster movement speed/can hide in natural cover
· longsword, shortsword, longbow, shortbow training
Dark Elf (Drow) (p25)
Larger, stronger females. Matriarchal society. Notorious for being chaotic/evil.
+2 DEX  +1 CHA
· learn Dancing Lights @ Lv1, Faerie Fire @ Lv5, and Darkness @ Lv7; all use INT and cast daily
· rapier, shortsword, hand-crossbow training
